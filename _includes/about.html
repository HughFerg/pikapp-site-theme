About Us

The Northeastern Associate Chapter of Pi Kappa Phi was founded in early 2019 and has since grown to 69 initiated brothers. We are a self-governed, social fraternity which prides itself on its commitment to the community and philanthropic endeavors. Much of our efforts are directed at our national philanthropic foundation, The Ability Experience, which creates experiences that promote the abilities of all people and works to eliminate labels that have unfairly come to define others. We also participate in many community service and fundraising initiatives, such as the annual Relay for Life fundraiser and other smaller events on campus.
